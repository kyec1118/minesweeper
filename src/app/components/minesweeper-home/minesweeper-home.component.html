Welcome to Minesweeper

Test your logic and luck in this classic puzzle game!

How to Play

  • Click on cells to reveal what's underneath
  • Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell
  • Right-click to flag cells you think contain mines
  • Clear all cells without mines to win!
